Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to services provided by adam health via our website and application. It describes the personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it. "Identifiable Personal Information" or "IPI" refers to any information that can personally identify you, such as an email address or photograph with unique features.

Information We Collect

adam health may collect personal information from you under certain circumstances when voluntarily provided on our website or application. This may include:

  1. User's Name
  2. Location/Address (optional, collected only if location permission is granted or entered in a text box)
  3. Phone Number (required for login and OTP verification)
  4. Profile Photo (optional)
  5. Healthcare Services and Preferences (information related to the services you seek)
  6. Photographs and Videos (as applicable)
  7. Facebook Permissions (optional, if the user chooses to share on their Facebook page)
  8. User Activities (such as showing interest in specific services or policies)

Our services are intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect or solicit IPI from individuals under 18. If we identify any such accounts, we may delete IPI and other user information associated with them.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information

We also collect non-personally identifiable information about visitor activity on our website, including operating system, visit date and time, referring URLs, internet service provider, browser type, device identifier, and pages visited. This information is collected through cookies or other analytics technologies and helps us improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information provided by you to deliver relevant services, improve your user experience, and tailor adam health’s content to meet your needs. Your personal information is shared with third parties only as described in this policy.

adam health may use, share, or disclose your IPI and/or usage data for purposes such as:

  • Fraud Prevention
  • Customer Service
  • Research and Service Personalization

Our third-party partners may use your IPI only as necessary under adam health agreements. We may also share IPI with affiliated companies under common control, ensuring these entities adhere to this policy.

Mandatory Sharing of Information

adam health may disclose your information in response to legal inquiries, judicial processes, or governmental requests. We may also disclose information if we suspect policy violations or to protect the rights or safety of any person or entity.

Cookies

Cookies are used on our website to enhance your experience. These small text files help us recognize returning users, making navigation more user-friendly. While cookies improve functionality, visitors concerned about them may decline cookies via browser settings, though some site features may be impacted.

Retention of Personal Information

We retain your information only as long as necessary for service fulfillment, business purposes, or legal compliance. Once information is outdated or no longer required, it will be deleted or destroyed. We may retain copies of certain data for internal record-keeping or regulatory compliance as needed.

Your Choices

You provide your personal information voluntarily. Should you choose to withdraw your consent, unsubscribe, or request deletion of your data, adam health provides clear instructions on how to do so.

Security

adam health takes the security of your information seriously, implementing administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your IPI. While we follow standard security protocols to protect against unauthorized access, we cannot guarantee complete protection from potential risks associated with digital information sharing.

Changes to This Policy

adam health reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be posted on this page and highlighted to ensure transparency.

By using adam health’s services and submitting any information, you agree to this Privacy Policy. For questions, please reach out to care@beyome.in
